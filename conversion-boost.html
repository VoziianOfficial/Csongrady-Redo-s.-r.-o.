Why it matters

More Traffic Is Not Always The Answer If The Page Cannot Convert

Advertising, SEO, and social media can bring attention, but weak page flow, unclear CTAs, or confusing forms can waste that attention before it becomes a lead.

Conversion Boost focuses on what happens after the visitor arrives: the message, proof, page rhythm, friction, and action path that decide whether someone moves forward.
