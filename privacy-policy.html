01

Overview

Csongrady Redo s. r. o. provides digital marketing, advertising, SEO, social media, web design, conversion optimization, and local SEO services. This website is intended to present our services and allow visitors to contact us.

We aim to handle personal information responsibly, transparently, and only for purposes that are relevant to communication, service inquiries, website operation, and basic business administration.

02

Information we collect

We may collect information that you provide directly when you contact us, submit a form, send an email, or request information about our services.

Contact information

Name, email address, phone number if provided, company name if provided, and other details you choose to share.

Project information

Service interests, website or campaign details, marketing goals, messages, and information needed to respond to your inquiry.

Website usage information

Basic technical data such as browser type, device information, approximate usage activity, and cookie preferences.

03

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, communicate with potential clients, provide service information, improve website usability, and manage normal business operations.

  • To respond to messages and service requests.
  • To understand which marketing service may be relevant to your inquiry.
  • To improve website performance, clarity, and user experience.
  • To maintain records related to communication and business administration.
  • To comply with applicable legal, accounting, or security obligations.
04

Cookies and similar technologies

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember cookie preferences, support basic site functionality, and understand general website performance.

You can accept or decline cookies through the cookie banner. Your choice is saved in your browser through local storage so the banner does not repeatedly appear. More details are available in our Cookie Policy.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when it is necessary to operate the website, respond to your request, work with trusted service providers, protect our legal interests, or comply with applicable law.

Any third-party service provider should only handle information for the relevant business or technical purpose and not for unrelated independent use.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of your personal information.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.
