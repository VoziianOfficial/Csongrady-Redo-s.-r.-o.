Overview

Cookies are small files or browser-based technologies that can store information on your device. They may help websites work correctly, remember preferences, and understand how visitors interact with pages.

On this website, cookies and local storage may be used to support the cookie banner, improve browsing experience, and understand general website performance.

02

Types of cookies we may use

The exact technologies used can vary depending on website setup, hosting, analytics tools, embedded features, or future improvements.

Essential cookies

These support basic website functionality, security, preference storage, and page operation.

Preference cookies

These help remember choices such as cookie banner decisions or interface preferences.

Analytics cookies

These may help understand general website performance, page usage, and visitor interaction patterns.

03

How we use cookies

Cookies and similar technologies may be used for limited and practical purposes connected with website use and communication.

  • To remember your cookie choice after you accept or decline the banner.
  • To support basic site functionality and page behavior.
  • To improve website performance, layout clarity, and user experience.
  • To understand general visitor interaction with website sections.
  • To support future service, contact, or analytics improvements.
04

Local storage

This website uses browser local storage to remember your cookie banner preference. For example, when you accept or decline the banner, that choice can be saved in your browser so the banner does not appear repeatedly on every page.

Local storage stays in your browser until it is cleared by you, removed by browser settings, or changed by future website updates.

This website may include links or features connected to third-party services, such as map links, email links, social links, hosting tools, analytics tools, or embedded functionality.

Third-party services may use their own cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. We do not control third-party cookie practices.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through the cookie banner when available, and you can also adjust cookie settings directly in your browser.

  • You can accept or decline cookies through the website banner.
  • You can clear cookies and local storage in your browser settings.
  • You can block or limit cookies through browser privacy controls.
  • Blocking some cookies may affect how certain website features work.
